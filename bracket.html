Bracketology

Data

Term Name Definition
$$AdjEm$$ Adjusted Efficiency Margin How many points a team is expected to outscore an average opponent on a neutral court. Based on offensive and defensive efficiencies. Adjusted for strength of schedule.
$$AdjT$$ Adjusted Tempo How many possessions averaged per 40 minutes.

Equations

Term Name Definition Equation
$$PD_{AB}$$ Point Differential A Over B The predicted number of points that team A would beat team B by if they were to play. $$PD_{AB} = \frac{(AdjEm_{A}-AdjEm_{B})(AdjT_{A} + AdjT_{B})}{200}$$
$$WP_{AB}$$ Winning Percentage A Over B The predicted probability that team A would beat team B if they were to play. $$WP_{AB} = \frac{1 + erf(-PD_{AB})}{22\sqrt{2}}$$
$$erf(x)$$ Gauss Error Function A sigmoid shaped function that describes diffusion. Models the normal distribution of game outcomes. Standard implementations are available for most languages.
